Surah Al-Alaq

The Clot • Makkah • 19 Verses
ٱقْرَأْ بِٱسْمِ رَبِّكَ ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَ
"Recite in the name of your Lord who created—"
Plain Understanding
The command is to read or recite, specifically invoking the name of the Sustainer (Rabb) who originated all creation.
Historical Context (Al-Qurtubi)
The first moment of Prophethood in the Cave of Hira. The Prophet ﷺ said "I am not a reciter," proving this knowledge is Divine, not learned.
Purification (Al-Ghazali)
Knowledge must be sought "in the name of your Lord." Knowledge for status is darkness; knowledge for Allah is Light.
Wisdom (Ibn Ata’illah)
"He who is illumined at the beginning, is illumined at the end." Start with His Name to guarantee the end.

خَلَقَ ٱلْإِنسَـٰنَ مِنْ عَلَقٍ
"Created man from a clinging substance."
Plain Understanding
Allah created humans from alaq—a suspended clot or leech-like substance.
Historical Context
Emphasizes humble origins against the pride of Quraish lineage.
Purification (Al-Jilani)
Cures Arrogance. You were a clot; do not be proud. If you don't cling to Allah, you will cling to the world.
Wisdom
"Your desire to be known is proof of your lack of truthfulness in servitude."
Human NatureHumility
ٱقْرَأْ وَرَبُّكَ ٱلْأَكْرَمُ
"Recite, and your Lord is the most Generous—"
Plain Understanding
Keep reciting, for your Lord is Al-Akram (Most Generous).
Historical Context
Reassurance to the Prophet ﷺ. He gives not because you are capable, but because He is Generous.
Purification
Cures Despair. Do not look at your flaws; look at His Generosity.
Attributes of AllahHope
ٱلَّذِى عَلَّمَ بِٱلْقَلَمِ
"Who taught by the pen—"
Plain Understanding
He taught humanity how to use the pen to record knowledge.
Purification
The heart should be like the pen: silent and moving only when the Writer (Allah) commands.
Wisdom
"He subjected everything to you, so that you might know His kindness."

عَلَّمَ ٱلْإِنسَـٰنَ مَا لَمْ يَعْلَمْ
"Taught man that which he knew not."
Plain Understanding
Humans are born knowing nothing; all knowledge is a gift.
Purification
Cures Intellectual Pride. Your intelligence is a loan.

كَلَّآ إِنَّ ٱلْإِنسَـٰنَ لَيَطْغَىٰٓ
أَن رَّءَاهُ ٱسْتَغْنَىٰ
"No! Indeed, man transgresses. Because he sees himself self-sufficient."
Historical Context
Revealed about Abu Jahl. He felt he didn't need God because of his wealth.
Purification
The root of Disbelief is "Istighna" (feeling you don't need God). Faith is realizing your poverty (Faqr).
Wisdom
"The source of every disobedience is satisfaction with one's self."
Human NatureArrogance
كَلَّا لَا تُطِعْهُ وَٱسْجُدْ وَٱقْتَرِب
"No! Do not obey him. But prostrate and draw near."
Plain Understanding
Disobey the tyrant; prostrate to Allah to get closer.
Purification
Sajdah (Prostration) is the cure. The lowest physical position is the highest spiritual position.
Wisdom
"He commanded you to pray not because He needs your service, but because He wants to bring you near."
