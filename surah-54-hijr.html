Plain Understanding
The majestic conclusion of the Surah. You do not worship God only when you feel good or when life is easy. Purification of the Self (Al-Ghazali)
"Hatta ya'tiyaka al-yaqeen" (Until certainty comes to you). "Al-Yaqeen" (Certainty) here is unanimously understood by scholars to mean Death, because death is the one reality that no atheist, philosopher, or king can deny. The ego seeks "arrival"—a point where worship is no longer required. God destroys this illusion. There is no retirement from servitude (Uboodiyyah). You must maintain the posture of a servant until the exact microsecond your soul is extracted from your body. Only then is the battle won.
