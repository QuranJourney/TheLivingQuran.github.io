Surah Al-Mumtahanah

She Who is Tested • Madinah • 13 Verses
Revealed strictly before the monumental Conquest of Makkah, this Surah actively dismantles the psychological and political loyalties of the believers. It establishes ironclad foreign policy, asserts the absolute supremacy of ideological brotherhood over biological family, and institutes the rigorous testing protocol for migrating believers.
Verse 1
يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ لَا تَتَّخِذُوا۟ عَدُوِّى وَعَدُوَّكُمْ أَوْلِيَآءَ تُلْقُونَ إِلَيْهِم بِٱلْمَوَدَّةِ وَقَدْ كَفَرُوا۟ بِمَا جَآءَكُم مِّنَ ٱلْحَقِّ يُخْرِجُونَ ٱلرَّسُولَ وَإِيَّاكُمْ ۙ أَن تُؤْمِنُوا۟ بِٱللَّهِ رَبِّكُمْ إِن كُنتُمْ خَرَجْتُمْ جِهَـٰدًۭا فِى سَبِيلِى وَٱبْتِغَآءَ مَرْضَاتِى ۚ تُسِرُّونَ إِلَيْهِم بِٱلْمَوَدَّةِ وَأَنَا۠ أَعْلَمُ بِمَآ أَخْفَيْتُمْ وَمَآ أَعْلَنتُمْ ۚ وَمَن يَفْعَلْهُ مِنكُمْ فَقَدْ ضَلَّ سَوَآءَ ٱلسَّبِيلِ
"O believers! Do not take My enemies and yours as trusted allies, showing them affection even though they deny what has come to you of the truth. They drove the Messenger and yourselves out of Mecca, simply for your belief in Allah, your Lord. If you truly emigrated to struggle in My cause and seek My pleasure, then do not take them as allies, disclosing secrets of the believers to the pagans out of affection for them, when I know best whatever you conceal and whatever you reveal. And whoever of you does this has truly strayed from the Right Way."
Plain Understanding
Allah aggressively intercepts an act of high treason, establishing an absolute zero-tolerance policy for geopolitical leaks. He explicitly forbids the believers from extending diplomatic affection or secret alliances to the pagan elite. Allah mathematically calculates their crimes: they aggressively rejected the divine truth, and violently exiled the Prophet and the believers simply for their monotheism. Allah violently strips the ego of its illusion of secrecy: attempting to secretly pass classified military intel to protect worldly interests is futile, because the Creator actively monitors the hidden architecture of the mind. Executing such treason actively derails the believer from the path of salvation.
Historical Context
This verse directly addresses the companion Hatib ibn Abi Balta'ah. As the Prophet (ﷺ) secretly prepared the massive army to conquer Makkah, Hatib wrote a classified letter to the Quraysh leadership warning them of the impending attack. He paid a woman to smuggle it in her hair. Hatib was not a hypocrite; he was a veteran of Badr. He committed this treason strictly because his unprotected family was stranded in Makkah, and he hoped the Quraysh would spare them in exchange for the intel. Allah informed the Prophet (ﷺ) via revelation, Ali (RA) intercepted the letter, and Allah revealed this verse to violently correct Hatib's skewed priorities.
Divine Wisdom (Ibn Ata'illah)
Ibn Ata'illah points out the psychological impossibility of dual loyalties. "Do not take My enemies as allies." You cannot claim to harbor absolute love for the King while simultaneously sending secret letters of affection to the King's active assassins. Hatib's ego convinced him he could balance his love for Allah with a pragmatic political move to save his family. Allah shattered this: loyalty to the Divine must actively supersede biological survival.

Verse 2
إِن يَثْقَفُوكُمْ يَكُونُوا۟ لَكُمْ أَعْدَآءًۭ وَيَبْسُطُوٓا۟ إِلَيْكُمْ أَيْدِيَهُمْ وَأَلْسِنَتَهُم بِٱلسُّوٓءِ وَوَدُّوا۟ لَوْ تَكْفُرُونَ
"If they gain the upper hand over you, they would be your open enemies, unleashing their hands and tongues to harm you, and wishing that you would abandon faith."
Plain Understanding
Allah violently deconstructs Hatib's political miscalculation. Hatib believed that doing a favor for the Quraysh would buy their mercy for his family. Allah exposes the absolute, toxic reality of the enemy's psychology: their hatred for Islam is terminal and un-negotiable. If the pagans ever gained tactical superiority, they would instantly violate any perceived political favor, actively weaponizing their hands (physical violence) and tongues (psychological torture) against the believers. Their ultimate, burning objective is not political peace, but the active eradication of the believers' faith.
Historical Context
The Quraysh had a thirteen-year track record of unspeakable brutality against the Muslims. They tortured Bilal, killed Sumayyah, and starved the Prophet's clan. Allah reminds the believers that signing a secret peace deal with a rabid wolf does not change the wolf's biological desire to consume you. The enemy's hostility is ideological, not situational.
Divine Wisdom (Al-Ghazali)
Al-Ghazali notes the delusion of attempting to appease an ideological enemy. The ego assumes that kindness will magically reform a deeply corrupted tyrant. Allah confirms that appeasing an enemy who inherently despises your truth only makes them bolder. They do not want your friendship; they explicitly "wish that you would abandon faith." Compromising your religion to gain their favor guarantees you will lose both.

Verse 3
لَن تَنفَعَكُمْ أَرْحَامُكُمْ وَلَآ أَوْلَـٰدُكُمْ ۚ يَوْمَ ٱلْقِيَـٰمَةِ يَفْصِلُ بَيْنَكُمْ ۚ وَٱللَّهُ بِمَا تَعْمَلُونَ بَصِيرٌۭ
"Neither your relatives nor children will benefit you on Judgment Day—He will decide between you all. For Allah is All-Seeing of what you do."
Plain Understanding
Allah systematically dismantles the emotional justification for treason. Hatib risked the entire survival of the Islamic state to protect his bloodline. Allah introduces the terrifying physics of the Hereafter: on the Day of Judgment, biological architecture is permanently vaporized. The relatives and children for whom you compromised divine law will actively flee from you; they possess absolutely zero capability to shield you from the Creator's wrath. Allah actively acts as the ultimate separator (Yafsilu), dividing families strictly by ideological obedience. He is continuously watching what you sacrifice the truth for.
Historical Context
When Hatib was caught, Umar ibn al-Khattab (RA) drew his sword, demanding to execute him for high treason. The Prophet (ﷺ) stayed Umar's hand, actively reminding him that Hatib fought at Badr, and Allah had guaranteed forgiveness for the Badr veterans. Allah used this incident not to execute Hatib, but to permanently legislate that family protection is an invalid defense for state treason.
Divine Wisdom (Al-Qurtubi)
Al-Qurtubi extracts the hierarchy of loyalty. Love for family is a biological instinct, but it must be violently subordinated to the love of Allah. If you commit a sin to enrich or protect your child, that exact child will testify against you on the Day of Judgment to save themselves. True love for your family is refusing to drag them and yourself into the Fire through illicit means.

Verse 4
قَدْ كَانَتْ لَكُمْ أُسْوَةٌ حَسَنَةٌۭ فِىٓ إِبْرَٰهِيمَ وَٱلَّذِينَ مَعَهُۥٓ إِذْ قَالُوا۟ لِقَوْمِهِمْ إِنَّا بُرَءَٰٓؤُا۟ مِنكُمْ وَمِمَّا تَعْبُدُونَ مِن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ كَفَرْنَا بِكُمْ وَبَدَا بَيْنَنَا وَبَيْنَكُمُ ٱلْعَدَٰوَةُ وَٱلْبَغْضَآءُ أَبَدًا حَتَّىٰ تُؤْمِنُوا۟ بِٱللَّهِ وَحْدَهُۥٓ إِلَّا قَوْلَ إِبْرَٰهِيمَ لِأَبِيهِ لَأَسْتَغْفِرَنَّ لَكَ وَمَآ أَمْلِكُ لَكَ مِنَ ٱللَّهِ مِن شَىْءٍۢ ۖ رَّبَّنَا عَلَيْكَ تَوَكَّلْنَا وَإِلَيْكَ أَنَبْنَا وَإِلَيْكَ ٱلْمَصِيرُ
"You already have an excellent example in Abraham and those with him, when they said to their people, We totally dissociate ourselves from you and shun whatever idols you worship besides Allah. We reject you. The enmity and hatred that has arisen between us and you will last until you believe in Allah alone. The only exception is when Abraham said to his father, I will seek forgiveness for you, adding, but I cannot protect you from Allah at all. The believers prayed, Our Lord! In You we trust. And to You we always turn. And to You is the final return."
Plain Understanding
Allah violently demands the believers recalibrate their loyalty by studying the absolute, uncompromising standard of Prophet Ibrahim (Abraham). Ibrahim actively executed Al-Walaa wal-Bara'a (Loyalty and Disavowal). He did not negotiate with his pagan society; he mathematically severed all ties, declaring permanent, active ideological enmity against his own people and their false gods until they submitted to monotheism. Allah highlights one strict exception: Ibrahim's initial promise to pray for his pagan father (a promise he later retracted when his father's hostility to Allah became clear). The verse concludes with the ultimate prayer of absolute surrender, anchoring total trust exclusively in the Almighty.
Historical Context
The Companions who emigrated from Makkah still felt deep, agonizing nostalgia and love for their pagan relatives left behind. By invoking Ibrahim—who was forced to abandon his own father and his homeland of Babylon for the sake of Tawhid—Allah validates their pain while demanding they match Ibrahim's ruthless prioritization of faith over bloodline.
Divine Wisdom (Ibn Ata'illah)
Ibn Ata'illah focuses on the surgical nature of disavowal. The heart cannot fully contain the Light of Allah until it is violently emptied of the love for idols (whether statues of stone or the idols of cultural approval). Ibrahim's greatness was not just smashing the physical idols with an axe; it was smashing the idol of social acceptance in his own heart, proudly embracing isolation to secure the company of the Creator.

Verse 5
رَبَّنَا لَا تَجْعَلْنَا فِتْنَةًۭ لِّلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ وَٱغْفِرْ لَنَا رَبَّنَآ ۖ إِنَّكَ أَنتَ ٱلْعَزِيزُ ٱلْحَكِيمُ
"Our Lord! Do not subject us to the persecution of the disbelievers. Forgive us, our Lord! You alone are truly the Almighty, All-Wise."
Plain Understanding
Allah records the continuation of Ibrahim’s profound prayer, establishing a vital theological petition for all future believers. He pleads to not be made a "Fitnah" (a test or trial) for the disbelievers. This is a highly strategic prayer: if the believers are militarily crushed, humiliated, or act corruptly, the disbelievers will arrogantly assume their false religion is superior because they hold the upper hand. Ibrahim asks Allah to grant the believers strength and victory, not out of ego, but specifically to prevent the tyrants from feeling validated in their disbelief.
Historical Context
As the Muslims prepared to march on Makkah, they were significantly outnumbered by the combined pagan forces of Arabia. If the Muslims were slaughtered during this campaign, the Quraysh would boast that the idols of the Ka'bah had destroyed the God of Muhammad (ﷺ). This prayer actively aligns military victory with the theological protection of Allah's Name.
Divine Wisdom (Al-Ghazali)
Al-Ghazali highlights the psychological depth of this supplication. When a believer suffers, the ego complains about the pain. But the illuminated heart of a Prophet worries about the theological implications of that pain. Ibrahim is not just asking to be saved from torture; he is actively asking Allah to protect the truth from being slandered by his defeat.

Verse 6
لَقَدْ كَانَ لَكُمْ فِيهِمْ أُسْوَةٌ حَسَنَةٌۭ لِّمَن كَانَ يَرْجُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ وَٱلْيَوْمَ ٱلْـَٔاخِرَ ۚ وَمَن يَتَوَلَّ فَإِنَّ ٱللَّهَ هُوَ ٱلْغَنِىُّ ٱلْحَمِيدُ
"You certainly have an excellent example in them for whoever has hope in Allah and the Last Day. But whoever turns away, then surely Allah alone is the Self-Sufficient, Praiseworthy."
Plain Understanding
Allah aggressively reiterates the mandate to mirror Ibrahim's uncompromising loyalty, but He adds a strict psychological filter: this standard of excellence is only accessible to the specific individual whose internal hope is radically tethered to the Creator and the Last Day. If a human being relies on worldly politics (like Hatib did), they will fail this standard. Allah then violently severs Himself from human validation. If the believers choose to turn away and maintain toxic alliances, Allah mathematically guarantees His own absolute independence (Al-Ghani). He actively needs zero human loyalty to sustain His Praiseworthy majesty.
Historical Context
By bookending the story of Ibrahim with the reminder of Allah's Self-Sufficiency, Allah removed any arrogance the early Muslims might have felt regarding their military sacrifices. You do not fight for Allah because Allah is weak and needs your sword; you fight for Allah because you are weak and desperately need His Paradise.
Divine Wisdom (Al-Qurtubi)
Al-Qurtubi deeply analyzes the attribute of Al-Ghani. The human ego operates on transactional relationships: "I helped you, now you owe me." Allah completely destroys this transaction. If every human on Earth committed high treason and worshipped idols, it would not decrease the kingdom of Allah by a single atom. We are commanded to be loyal strictly to save ourselves from the Fire, not to protect the Throne.

Verse 7
۞ عَسَى ٱللَّهُ أَن يَجْعَلَ بَيْنَكُمْ وَبَيْنَ ٱلَّذِينَ عَادَيْتُم مِّنْهُم مَّوَدَّةًۭ ۚ وَٱللَّهُ قَدِيرٌۭ ۚ وَٱللَّهُ غَفُورٌۭ رَّحِيمٌۭ
"In time, Allah may bring about goodwill between you and those of them you now hold as enemies. For Allah is Most Capable. And Allah is All-Forgiving, Most Merciful."
Plain Understanding
Immediately after commanding the violent ideological severing of ties, Allah brilliantly introduces the fluidity of human destiny and divine intervention. He actively prophesies a massive geopolitical shift. The very pagans the Muslims are currently commanded to hate for the sake of Allah may eventually be guided to the truth. When Allah actively changes their hearts, the bitter enmity will instantaneously transform into deep, biological and spiritual brotherhood ("goodwill"). Allah asserts His capability (Qadir) to execute this impossible psychological alchemy, backed by His infinite forgiveness.
Historical Context
This verse was a stunning prophecy of the impending Conquest of Makkah. Men like Abu Sufyan, Ikrimah ibn Abi Jahl, and Safwan ibn Umayyah were the absolute worst butchers of the Muslim state. The Companions hated them with a burning passion. Yet, within months of this revelation, Makkah surrendered, these exact warlords accepted Islam, and they became some of the most fiercely loyal and beloved generals in the Muslim army. Allah actively overwrote a decade of hatred with an instant injection of faith.
Divine Wisdom (Ibn Ata'illah)
Ibn Ata'illah notes the profound balance of Islamic enmity. A believer hates an enemy only because of their disbelief, not out of personal ego or racial animosity. Therefore, the hatred is entirely conditional. The exact microsecond the enemy utters the Shahada (declaration of faith), the condition vanishes, and the believer is religiously obligated to actively love the man who tried to kill him yesterday. You hate the disease, but you must always leave the door open for the Patient's cure.

Verse 8
لَّا يَنْهَىٰكُمُ ٱللَّهُ عَنِ ٱلَّذِينَ لَمْ يُقَٰتِلُوكُمْ فِى ٱلدِّينِ وَلَمْ يُخْرِجُوكُم مِّن دِيَـٰرِكُمْ أَن تَبَرُّوهُمْ وَتُقْسِطُوٓا۟ إِلَيْهِمْ ۚ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ يُحِبُّ ٱلْمُقْسِطِينَ
"Allah does not forbid you from dealing kindly and fairly with those who have neither fought nor driven you out of your homes. Surely Allah loves those who are fair."
Plain Understanding
Allah establishes the definitive, absolute baseline for Islamic foreign policy and interfaith relations. He explicitly categorizes non-Muslims. For those who actively abstain from religious warfare and geographical expulsion, Allah does not merely "permit" tolerance; He actively encourages profound kindness (Birr) and absolute, mathematical justice (Qist). He mathematically binds His divine love directly to those who execute this fairness, proving that ideological separation does not authorize cruelty.
Historical Context
As geopolitical tensions flared and the Muslims severed ties with the treacherous Makkan elite, some Companions assumed they had to cut off all pagan relatives, including peaceful ones. Asma bint Abi Bakr's pagan mother visited her in Madinah seeking help. Asma refused to let her in until she asked the Prophet (ﷺ). This verse descended, explicitly commanding Asma to show her mother exceptional kindness and financial support, proving Islam actively protects peaceful biological ties.
Divine Wisdom (Ibn Ata'illah)
Ibn Ata'illah highlights the absolute distinction between theological loyalty and human compassion. A believer must actively disavow pagan theology in their heart, but must actively execute supreme kindness with their hands. Treating a peaceful non-believer with harshness is not a sign of piety; it is a violent violation of Allah's love for justice, turning the believer into an oppressor.

Verse 9
إِنَّمَا يَنْهَىٰكُمُ ٱللَّهُ عَنِ ٱلَّذِينَ قَٰتَلُوكُمْ فِى ٱلدِّينِ وَأَخْرَجُوكُم مِّن دِيَـٰرِكُمْ وَظَـٰهَرُوا۟ عَلَىٰٓ إِخْرَاجِكُمْ أَن تَوَلَّوْهُمْ ۚ وَمَن يَتَوَلَّهُمْ فَأُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ هُمُ ٱلظَّـٰلِمُونَ
"Allah only forbids you from befriending those who have fought you for your faith, driven you out of your homes, or supported others in doing so. And whoever takes them as friends, then it is they who are the true wrongdoers."
Plain Understanding
Allah explicitly isolates the target of the ideological and political boycott. The strict prohibition of alliance is mathematically limited to active combatants: those who orchestrate religious persecution, execute physical expulsion, or provide logistical and financial support to the oppressors. To offer friendship, political cover, or alliance to this specific demographic is mathematically classified as supreme oppression (Dhulm). You cannot ally with the active murderers of the believers without becoming complicit in their murder.
Historical Context
This verse codified the exact parameter of the state's boycott against the Quraysh elite and the Jewish tribes that broke their treaties. It separated the passive disbeliever from the active enemy combatant, establishing a sophisticated, highly nuanced foreign policy rather than a blanket, emotional war against all non-Muslims.
Divine Wisdom (Al-Qurtubi)
Al-Qurtubi notes the active mechanics of oppression. By befriending an active oppressor, you actively validate their oppression, providing them the psychological and political fuel to continue slaughtering the innocent. Thus, the "friend" of the tyrant is structurally indistinguishable from the tyrant himself in the court of Divine Justice.

Verse 10
يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوٓا۟ إِذَا جَآءَكُمُ ٱلْمُؤْمِنَـٰتُ مُهَـٰجِرَٰتٍۢ فَٱمْتَحِنُوهُنَّ ۖ ٱللَّهُ أَعْلَمُ بِإِيمَـٰنِهِنَّ ۖ فَإِنْ عَلِمْتُمُوهُنَّ مُؤْمِنَـٰتٍۢ فَلَا تَرْجِعُوهُنَّ إِلَى ٱلْكُفَّارِ ۖ لَا هُنَّ حِلٌّۭ لَّهُمْ وَلَا هُمْ يَحِلُّونَ لَهُنَّ ۖ وَءَاتُوهُم مَّآ أَنفَقُوا۟ ۚ وَلَا جُنَاحَ عَلَيْكُمْ أَن تَنكِحُوهُنَّ إِذَآ ءَاتَيْتُمُوهُنَّ أُجُورَهُنَّ ۚ وَلَا تُمْسِكُوا۟ بِعِصَمِ ٱلْكَوَافِرِ وَسْـَٔلُوا۟ مَآ أَنفَقْتُمْ وَلْيَسْـَٔلُوا۟ مَآ أَنفَقُوا۟ ۚ ذَٰلِكُمْ حُكْمُ ٱللَّهِ ۖ يَحْكُمُ بَيْنَكُمْ ۚ وَٱللَّهُ عَلِيمٌ حَكِيمٌۭ
"O believers! When the believing women come to you as emigrants, test their intentions—their faith is best known to Allah—and if you find them to be believers, then do not send them back to the disbelievers. These women are not lawful wives for the disbelievers, nor are the disbelievers lawful husbands for them. But repay the disbelievers whatever dowries they had paid. And there is no blame on you if you marry these women as long as you pay them their dowries. And do not hold on to marriage with polytheistic women. But demand repayment of whatever dowries you had paid, and let the disbelievers do the same. That is the judgment of Allah—He judges between you. And Allah is All-Knowing, All-Wise."
Plain Understanding
Allah violently shatters the pre-existing Treaty of Hudaybiyyah regarding migrating women, issuing an absolute legal override. When a woman escapes to Madinah claiming Islam, the state must actively execute an interrogation (the "Test") to ensure she is not a spy or fleeing a domestic dispute. Once verified, Allah permanently outlaws returning her to the pagans, officially dissolving her previous pagan marriage. However, to maintain absolute geopolitical justice, the Islamic state is actively commanded to refund the pagan husband's original dowry. Simultaneously, Muslim men are strictly commanded to unilaterally divorce any pagan wives left behind in Makkah, while demanding their own dowry refunds. Allah declares this the unyielding, mathematically precise judgment of the Creator.
Historical Context
The Treaty of Hudaybiyyah explicitly stated that anyone fleeing Makkah to Madinah must be returned. When vulnerable Muslim women like Umm Kulthum bint Uqba escaped to Madinah, their pagan families arrived demanding their extradition. The Prophet (ﷺ) was bound by the treaty until Allah revealed this verse, explicitly exempting women from the extradition clause to protect them from physical torture, while aggressively enforcing massive financial reparations to honor the spirit of the treaty.
Divine Wisdom (Al-Ghazali)
Al-Ghazali focuses on the terrifying precision of divine justice. Even when dealing with a rabid, bloodthirsty enemy, Allah refuses to allow the believers to steal their wealth. By actively refunding the dowries of the pagan men, the Islamic state proves that its actions are driven exclusively by theological purity, not financial theft. True justice demands paying your enemy what they are mathematically owed.

Verse 11
وَإِن فَاتَكُمْ شَىْءٌۭ مِّنْ أَزْوَٰجِكُمْ إِلَى ٱلْكُفَّارِ فَعَاقَبْتُمْ فَـَٔاتُوا۟ ٱلَّذِينَ ذَهَبَتْ أَزْوَٰجُهُم مِّثْلَ مَآ أَنفَقُوا۟ ۚ وَٱتَّقُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ ٱلَّذِىٓ أَنتُم بِهِۦ مُؤْمِنُونَ
"And if any of your wives desert you to the disbelievers, and later you take spoils from them, then pay those whose wives have gone, the equivalent of whatever dowry they had paid. And be mindful of Allah, in Whom you believe."
Plain Understanding
Allah establishes an active, state-sponsored financial compensation mechanism for Muslim victims of marital apostasy. If a Muslim man’s wife officially defects, abandoning Islam to join the pagan enemy, and the pagans refuse to refund his dowry, the Islamic state is actively obligated to reimburse the grieving husband directly out of the public treasury (spoils of war). Allah mathematically ensures that the believer suffers no financial loss for his adherence to the truth, instantly tethering this financial law to the continuous mindfulness (Taqwa) of the Almighty.
Historical Context
After the revelation commanding the divorce of pagan wives, some women actively apostatized and fled back to Makkah. The Quraysh, acting as hostile combatants, naturally refused to send the dowry refunds back to Madinah. To prevent the Muslim men from bearing the crushing weight of both a broken family and stolen wealth, the Prophet (ﷺ) actively compensated them from the state's military acquisitions.

Verse 12
يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلنَّبِىُّ إِذَا جَآءَكَ ٱلْمُؤْمِنَـٰتُ يُبَايِعْنَكَ عَلَىٰٓ أَن لَّا يُشْرِكْنَ بِٱللَّهِ شَيْـًۭٔا وَلَا يَسْرِقْنَ وَلَا يَزْنِينَ وَلَا يَقْتُلْنَ أَوْلَـٰدَهُنَّ وَلَا يَأْتِينَ بِبُهْتَـٰنٍۢ يَفْتَرِينَهُۥ بَيْنَ أَيْدِيهِنَّ وَأَرْجُلِهِنَّ وَلَا يَعْصِينَكَ فِى مَعْرُوفٍۢ ۙ فَبَايِعْهُنَّ وَٱسْتَغْفِرْ لَهُنَّ ٱللَّهَ ۖ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ غَفُورٌۭ رَّحِيمٌۭ
"O Prophet! When the believing women come to you, pledging to you that they will neither associate anything with Allah in worship, nor steal, nor fornicate, nor kill their children, nor falsely attribute illegitimate children to their husbands, nor disobey you in what is right, then accept their pledge, and ask Allah to forgive them. Surely Allah is All-Forgiving, Most Merciful."
Plain Understanding
Allah codifies the exact, active parameters of the "Test" (Al-Mumtahanah). The Prophet (ﷺ) is commanded to extract an absolute, six-point legal and spiritual oath of allegiance (Bay'ah) from the migrating women. They must actively swear to absolute monotheism, absolute financial integrity (no stealing), absolute physical chastity (no fornication), absolute protection of life (no infanticide), absolute marital honesty (no fabricating lineage), and absolute geopolitical and religious obedience to the Prophet's just commands. Once the oath is verbally executed, the Prophet is commanded to officially accept them into the state and actively petition the Almighty for their past forgiveness.
Historical Context
Following the Conquest of Makkah, hundreds of women came to pledge allegiance. The Prophet (ﷺ) stood on Mount Safa and administered this exact oath. Hind bint Utbah, the woman who had previously mutilated the body of the Prophet's uncle Hamza, came disguised. As the Prophet read the conditions, she audaciously argued with him point by point out of aristocratic pride, but ultimately submitted, took the oath, and was entirely forgiven by the Prophet (ﷺ) under the active mandate of this verse.
Divine Wisdom (Ibn Ata'illah)
Ibn Ata'illah observes that the oath systematically dismantles the six primary pillars of pre-Islamic corruption. It attacks the corruption of the soul (polytheism), the economy (theft), the family (fornication and false lineage), the sanctity of life (infanticide), and the political structure (disobedience). True Islam is not a passive feeling; it is the active, signed, and executed commitment to completely re-engineer your biological and social behavior.

Verse 13
يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ لَا تَتَوَلَّوْا۟ قَوْمًا غَضِبَ ٱللَّهُ عَلَيْهِمْ قَدْ يَئِسُوا۟ مِنَ ٱلْـَٔاخِرَةِ كَمَا يَئِسَ ٱلْكُفَّارُ مِنْ أَصْحَـٰبِ ٱلْقُبُورِ
"O believers! Do not ally yourselves with a people Allah is displeased with. They already have no hope for the Hereafter, just like the disbelievers lying in their graves."
Plain Understanding
Allah violently seals the Surah with an absolute, terrifying conclusion on loyalty. He repeats and mathematically finalizes the command from Verse 1: any active alliance with a faction that has incurred the active wrath of the Creator is strictly forbidden. He diagnoses the terminal psychological state of the enemy: they possess absolutely zero hope for the Hereafter. They are biologically alive, but spiritually, they are as utterly dead, disconnected, and hopeless as the rotting corpses of the pagans already buried in the dirt. To ally with them is to chain yourself to a corpse.
Historical Context
Some poor Muslims in Madinah maintained secret economic alliances with the Jewish tribes (who had incurred divine wrath for their treason) to secure loans and food during times of famine. Allah violently severed these ties, warning the believers that seeking physical survival by attaching themselves to a spiritually dead, cursed demographic would actively drag them into the exact same grave of despair.
Divine Wisdom (Al-Ghazali)
Al-Ghazali isolates the terrifying metaphor of the "graves." When the ego seeks an alliance with the corrupt, it assumes they possess power, wealth, or vitality. Allah actively strips away the physical illusion. In the unseen reality, the tyrant sitting on a throne is indistinguishable from a skeleton rotting in a tomb. Both are completely devoid of divine light. You cannot extract life by hugging a corpse.
