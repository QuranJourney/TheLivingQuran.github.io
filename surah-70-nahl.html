Verse 125
ٱدْعُ إِلَىٰ سَبِيلِ رَبِّكَ بِٱلْحِكْمَةِ وَٱلْمَوْعِظَةِ ٱلْحَسَنَةِ ۖ وَجَـٰدِلْهُم بِٱلَّتِى هِىَ أَحْسَنُ ۚ إِنَّ رَبَّكَ هُوَ أَعْلَمُ بِمَن ضَلَّ عَن سَبِيلِهِۦ ۖ وَهُوَ أَعْلَمُ بِٱلْمُهْتَدِينَ
"Actively invite to the path of your Lord with profound wisdom and beautifully excellent instruction! And fiercely debate with them in exactly the way that is absolute best! Indeed, your Lord is vastly more knowing of exactly who has strayed completely from His path, and He is vastly more knowing of the perfectly guided!"
Plain Understanding
The Absolute Golden Rule of Da'wah (Preaching)! You cannot scream Islam into people's hearts. God commands three layers of communication: 1) "Hikmah" (Wisdom: knowing exactly *what* to say, *when* to say it, and who you are talking to). 2) "Maw'izah Hasanah" (Beautiful, emotionally intelligent preaching that heals, not hurts). 3) If you absolutely must debate, use the "Ahsan" (the most elegant, respectful, and logically flawless method possible). Do your job beautifully, and leave the results to God!

Verse 126
وَإِنْ عَاقَبْتُمْ فَعَاقِبُوا۟ بِمِثْلِ مَا عُوقِبْتُم بِهِۦ ۖ وَلَئِن صَبَرْتُمْ لَهُوَ خَيْرٌۭ لِّلصَّـٰبِرِينَ
"And if you physically punish [and retaliate], then punish strictly with the exact equivalent of exactly what you were violently punished with! But if you actively remain perfectly patient - it is absolutely, definitively vastly better for the intensely patient!"
Plain Understanding
The flawless balance of Justice and Mercy! God gives humans the absolute legal right to retaliate against someone who harms them, but the retaliation must be mathematically identical (no escalating the violence!). However, God immediately offers the VIP upgrade: If you swallow your pride, let go of the anger, and have "Sabr" (forgiveness/patience), God guarantees that the cosmic payout for that restraint will be vastly better than the satisfaction of revenge!

Verse 128
إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ مَعَ ٱلَّذِينَ ٱتَّقَوا۟ وَّٱلَّذِينَ هُم مُّحْسِنُونَ
"Indeed, Allah is exclusively, intimately WITH exactly those who fiercely protect themselves [in God-consciousness], and exactly those who are doers of absolute supreme excellence!"
Plain Understanding
The magnificent, comforting close to the Surah! God is not just watching from a distance. He is "Ma'a" (actively, intimately, and powerfully WITH) you! But He only grants this VIP protection and companionship to two types of people: 1) The Muttaqeen (those who actively block sin from entering their hearts), and 2) The Muhsineen (those who don't just do the bare minimum, but operate with radical beauty, generosity, and excellence in everything they do).
