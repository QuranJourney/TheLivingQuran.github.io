Surah Ad-Dhariyat

The Winnowing Winds • Makkah • 60 Verses
A visually explosive, fast-paced revelation that swears by the massive forces of the atmosphere to prove the certainty of Resurrection. It completely destroys human financial anxiety by declaring that true provision is locked securely in the heavens.
Verse 1
وَٱلذَّٰرِيَـٰتِ ذَرْوًۭا
"By the violently scattering, winnowing winds,"
Plain Understanding
The Surah opens with a massive, cinematic oath! "Ad-Dhariyat" refers to the hyper-speed winds that violently scatter dust, pollen, and seeds across the earth. God points to the invisible but overwhelmingly powerful physics of the atmosphere.
